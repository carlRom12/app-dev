### **The Long-Awaited Matchup**

> The film is the first of a two-part finale project for the Haikyu!! anime series. It centers entirely on the highly anticipated match between **Karasuno High** and their long-time rivals, **Nekoma High**.

### **Karasuno's Key Players**

1.  **Shoyo Hinata:** The optimistic, short-statured protagonist with incredible jumping power.
2.  **Tobio Kageyama:** The genius setter, often called the "King of the Court."
3.  **Tadashi Yamaguchi:** The pinch server who practices diligently to contribute to the team.

### **The "Dumpster Battle"**

-   The rivalry between Karasuno and Nekoma is famously nicknamed the "**Dumpster Battle**" because of the animals associated with their school names: crows (Karasuno) and cats (Nekoma).
-   This match has been a dream for the coaches and players for decades, finally happening on the grand stage of the **Spring High National Tournament**.

### **A Quote from the Film**

`"We're gonna be playing the game of our lives."`
